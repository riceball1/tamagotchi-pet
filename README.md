# Tamagotchi Pet


A simple classic Japanese game built with next.js and phaser 3


## How to play tamagotchi

- Take care of your alien pet
- A pet grows into several life stages:
    - Egg
    - Baby
    - Child
    - Teen
    - Adult
    - Senior

### How to care for your pet

- feed 
- light
- medicine
- toilet
- health meter
- discipline
- attention
